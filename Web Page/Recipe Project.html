Southern Buttermilk Biscuits

These tender and moist biscuits are easy to make, and come out perfectly golden brown!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of unbleached, all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of kosher salt
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 cup of buttermilk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Combine the dry ingredients into a bowl
  3. Cut the butter into chunks and cut the flour until it resembles course meal
  4. Add the buttermilk and mix JUST until combined
  5. Turn the dough onto a floured board
  6. Gently, pat the dough down until it is about 1/2 an inch thick, then fold the dough about five times and press down until the dough is about an inch thick
  7. Use a round cutter to cut the dough
  8. Place the rounds on a cookie sheet
  9. Bake for about 10-12 minutes.

Enjoy!
